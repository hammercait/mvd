MV Dozer

Seattle, Washington

Walkthrough

M/V Dozer is a very spacious boat due to its 23.5’ beam. Both the main salon and enclosed sky lounge are full beam which makes M/V Dozer’s interior very large. As you enter the vessels’ starboard foyer, you have the option of going forward to her airy country kitchen, aft to her main salon and dining area or up to her enclosed sky lounge.

Forward in the sky lounge is the helm station with a large raised bench seat for viewing while underway. Aft of this bench seat is informal seating, desk with computer and a wet bar. The aft double doors lead out to a spacious deck for sunning and seating. A hot tub and Traeger wood pellet BBQ grill are located on the starboard side of this deck. Behind this aft deck is a centerline davit, 17’ Nouvarania and 2 Hobie canoes.

M/V Dozer’s dining area (for 8) is located in the aft starboard corner of the main salon, with her salon seating area forward to port along with an entertainment center incorporating surround sound and a 35” TV. Adjacent to the entertainment center and located off the port foyer is the day head. Aft of this area portside, is a full wet bar with an etched glass bulkhead. Aft of the salon through double swinging doors is a huge aft deck with a back bench seat and a table for El Fresco dining. The aft deck leads down to a swim platform through dual stairs. Forward on the bow is a large sun pad.

The lower level guest accommodations consist of a full beam master with his and her heads forward away from the engine room. Moving aft of the master portside is a guest stateroom with a double bed. Starboard aft is the VIP, with a queen-size bed. The fourth stateroom is aft port with twin berths. Both the VIP and the twin staterooms aft are separated from the engine room by their ensuite heads for engine room noise reduction.
